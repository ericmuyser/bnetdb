About
Initial game list and search list are up to 30 minutes old. Active game lists are updated every 5-10 seconds. Data may not necessilary be up-to-date. Considering the delay, games may be full by the time they appear in the list. Games are not removed upon becoming full, or do not exist anymore. This is in order to allow searching history.

There is a total of 144 lists. Therefore, lists are actived based on demand. So far 4 lists are activated: D2 LoD USWSCL Hell, D2 LoD USESCL Normal, D2 LoD USESCL Hell, and D2 LoD EUSCL Hell. If you feel a list should be activated, please contact us.
